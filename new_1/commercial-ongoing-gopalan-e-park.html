Gopalan E-Park : Koorgalli Industrial Area, Mysore

Gopalan E-Park is strategically located in Koorgalli Industrial Area which proffers IT/ITES SEZ office spaces. Located in Mysore’s fastest developing growth corridors, the software park connects the city’s IT hubs. E-Park is envisioned to be a flexible and sustainable workplace, designed to provideworld-class infrastructure and bracing environment that fosters innovation and collaboration.

Project Overview

  • Location - 3rd Phase, Koorgalli Industrial Area, Koorgalli Village, Ilawala Hobli, Mysore
  • E-Park – IT/ITES SEZ Software Technology Park
  • Project Development - 28 acres
  • Total Blocks – 5 blocks for office space and a block dedicated for the cafeteria
  • Total built-up area - 50, 56,000 sq.ft.
  • No. of floors - 2B + G + 10 UF
  • Average floor plate - 60,000 sq.ft.

Project Traits

  • Water requirement for the proposed project will be sourced from KIADB
  • Flushing water requirement will be met by the STP Treated Water.
  • The energy requirement will be supplied by CHESCOM
    • .
  • Generator Sets (Dual fuel – Diesel & CNG) will be used as a power back-up source.
  • Solar powered lights will be provided for external lighting and common area lightings such as a walkway, terrace,and main gate areas.
  • Use of high efficiency LED and other energy efficient devices.
  • Fire safety norms are followed.

Other Features

  • Security 24x7
  • Clubhouse
  • Gym
  • Indoor games
  • Cafeteria
  • Creche
  • Convenience store
  • CCTV
  • 100% power backup
  • Leisure area and open space to relax
  • Ample car parking
  • Eco-friendly setup

Strategic Location

  • 13 km from Mysuru city Railway Station
  • 28 km from Mysuru Airport.
  • 0.6 km from Koorgalli bus stop
